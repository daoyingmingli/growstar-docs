Sparkles App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 20, 2026

Thank you for using Sparkles App. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting the personal information of you and your children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

Important Note: Sparkles App is an app designed for parents. We do not collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. All child-related data is managed by parents or guardians.

1. Information Collection

1.1 Information We Collect

  • User-Generated Data: Content you create while using the app, including child profiles (name, avatar, birthday), behavior records, wish settings, tags, and templates.
  • Device Information: We may collect information related to your device, such as device type, operating system version, app version, and device identifiers (such as UUID).
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use the app, such as feature usage frequency and interaction patterns, to improve the app experience.
  • Voice Data: If you use the voice input feature, voice data is processed locally on your device and is not sent to our servers.

1.2 Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or phone number unless you voluntarily provide it (such as when contacting us).
  • We do not collect precise location information.
  • We do not collect sensitive personal information such as health data or financial information.

2. Information Use

2.1 How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • To process your requests and transactions
  • To send service-related notifications (such as update announcements)
  • To analyze usage trends to improve user experience
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activities

2.2 Data Storage and Synchronization

We use Apple's CloudKit service to provide data synchronization functionality. When you enable iCloud synchronization, your data is securely stored on Apple's iCloud servers. We do not have access to your data stored in iCloud.

3. Information Sharing

We will not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties unless:

  • We have your explicit consent
  • Required or permitted by law (such as to comply with a court order)
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety

3.1 Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple CloudKit: Used for data synchronization and storage. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit: Used for processing subscription purchases. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Speech Framework: Used for voice recognition, processing occurs locally on the device.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect your information:

  • All data is stored encrypted on the device
  • iCloud synchronization uses Apple's end-to-end encryption
  • Regularly update security measures
  • Data is not stored on our own servers

Despite these measures, please understand that no security measures are perfect, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

5. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: Request copies of your personal information that we hold
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: Request restriction of processing your information
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided below.

6. Children's Privacy

Sparkles App is an app designed for parents to help them manage and motivate their children's growth. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. All child-related data is created and managed by parents or guardians.

If you believe we have collected personally identifiable information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete that information.

7. Data Retention

We only retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the service:

  • Your app data remains on your device until you choose to delete it
  • If using iCloud synchronization, data remains in your iCloud account until you delete it
  • You can delete all data in the app at any time

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you through in-app notifications or other appropriate means. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
