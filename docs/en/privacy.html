GrowStar Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. Data We Collect

The app does not actively collect personally identifiable information. We may process:

  • Parent-entered children behavior records, wishes, points, tags, etc.
  • Device information (app version, system version, device model for analytics and crash reporting)

2. Data Storage

All data is stored in the user's personal iCloud CloudKit private database. Developers cannot access or share it.

3. Children Data

Children's data is only entered by parents. The app does not collect, track, or share children's information.

4. Data Use

Data is used only for:

  • Providing core app functionality (behavior tracking, statistics)
  • Syncing across user's devices
  • Validating subscription benefits
  • Optimizing app experience

5. Data Deletion

Users can delete all data in the app. Deletion is permanent and cannot be recovered.

6. Payment Information

All subscription payments are handled by Apple. Developers do not have access to payment information.

7. Contact

For questions, contact: support@growstar.app
