GrowStar Subscription Terms

Last updated: 2025

1. Subscription Service

Subscribers gain full access to behavior tracking, analytics, and wish management features.

2. Renewal and Cancellation

  • Subscriptions are auto-renewable via Apple ID
  • Users can cancel through Apple ID settings
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle

3. Payment and Refunds

All payments are processed by Apple. Refunds are handled according to Apple policy. Developers cannot access payment info.

4. Data and Benefits

Subscription data is stored in iCloud CloudKit and will not be deleted if subscription ends. Premium benefits are not retained after cancellation.
